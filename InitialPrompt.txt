Student Tuition Management System
Objective

Design and implement a full-stack web application for managing my private tutoring business. The system should automate student management, scheduling, attendance tracking, monthly billing, payment tracking, and parent communication.

The solution should be production-quality, modular, maintainable, and designed with future enhancements in mind.

Business Background

I teach students privately.

Teaching schedule:

Sundays:
8:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Lunch break approximately 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM
Weekdays:
Monday through Friday
5:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Each class is normally:

1 hour
Begins at the start of an hour
Recurs weekly

Examples:

Sunday 8 AM

Sunday 9 AM

Wednesday 6 PM

Thursday 7 PM

Most students attend once every week.

Sometimes parents enroll two or more children.

In that case the sessions are usually consecutive.

Example:

Student A: Sunday 9–10

Student B: Sunday 10–11

Subjects

Primary subject:

Mathematics

Occasionally:

Physics
Computer Science
Statistics

The design should allow additional subjects to be added without schema changes.

Rates are NOT fixed.

Grade
Subject
Individual agreement
Discounts

Therefore:

Do not hardcode rates.

Create a flexible pricing model.

Each student should have one or more pricing records with effective dates.

Historical rates must never be overwritten.

Examples:

Jan–May: $45/session

Special discount:
